How to Buy Freemoon

A step by step guide on how to buy Freemoon from Pancakeswap.

Step 1: Get your Wallet

We recommend using Trust Wallet.
For iOS users, get it here.
For Android Users, get it here.
Note: Keep your seed phrase and private keys in a safe location. WE WILL NEVER ASK YOU FOR THESE AND DO NOT SHARE THEM WITH ANYONE!

Step 2: Add Freemoon as an option to the Wallet

Tap the icon in the top right hand corner and search for "Freemoon". If you cannot find “Freemoon”, you can search for it using the "Add Custom Token" button.

Change the Network to "Smart Chain". Copy the contract address below and put it in the “Contract Address” box.

Address
0x8076c74c5e3f5852037f31ff0093eeb8c8add8d3

Next, put "Freemoon" as the name, and the symbol as FM. Decimals will be 9. Click "Done" at the top and you should now have Freemoon added to your wallet!

Step 3: Buy "Smart Chain" (BNB)

Tap on "Smart Chain" on the main screen of Trust Wallet, then tap “Buy” in the top right hand corner.

Note: On the wallet main screen the tokens “BNB” and “Smart Chain” have the same token symbol BNB. You want the “Smart Chain” BNB for this step.

Step 4: Swap BSC for Freemoon!

For iOS users, visit https://exchange.pancakeswap.finance/#/swap in your browser, select “Connect” in the top right hand corner. In the “Connect to a Wallet” menu, choose “WalletConnect” then choose “Trust” and click “Connect” when prompted. Return to your browser and you will see your wallet has been connected.

In the “From” tab you can select BNB and you will see your balance reflected on the Pancakeswap website. On the “To” tab choose “Freemoon”.

After setting the slippage percentage from the icon in the top right hand corner, choose “Swap” and you will be directed to your Trust Wallet where you will confirm the transaction. In your browser, you should see the transaction submitted and the option to view the transaction status on BscScan.

For Android users, after you have purchased your Smart Chain BNB, Go to the “DApps” section at the bottom of the main screen.

Open the “DApps” section, and locate and open PancakeSwap under “Exchanges”. Connect your Trust Wallet in the top-right.

Go to the "Exchange" box, click the icon and set the slippage to your preferred slippage rate (the acceptable price increase range you are willing to accept if prices of your coin are increasing by the minute). Increasing your “Transaction deadline” can give your transaction a higher chance of clearing.

With your slippage set, choose how much BNB you want to trade for Freemoon and click “Swap”. After confirming the transaction, you will have the option to view the transcation status on BscScan.

Once the transaction has been cleared, you should have Freemoon in your Trust Wallet! Congratulations! You have played a small part in the freedom network effect!
